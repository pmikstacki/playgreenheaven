Project Timeline

Althought we make this game in our free time, we want to present you certain milestones in the cycle of development

5 April 2019

First Semi-public Developer Build

The first publicly available build will be just for pure demonstrational purposes. With this build we want to achieve basic functionality without actually implementing game's story. 
To have the chance to try it, signup for our newsletter!
We don't spam!

15 August 2019

First public alpha!

In this milestone, we are half-way into implementing the story, although you might experience some bugs!

16 December 2019

First Beta Build

This time the story is fully incorporated into our game, they may be some glitches but the whole game is supposed to work! (We hope)

24 January 2020

Official Release of the game

After a year of development the game hits the major game distribution platforms such as GOG, G2A and Steam. With your help we can make it on the steam store!
