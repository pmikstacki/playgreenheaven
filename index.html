Edutaining Harm Reduction Game

Explore the amazing city of weedwood - lonely island in the middle of nowhere. 
People come here to relax, party and... take drugs.
Your mission is to supply them with the best stuff you can find!

What this is all about?

Harm reduction is very important.
Many people from various ages are trying psychodelics and other narcotics.
They were doing it, they are doing it and they will continue to do it.
However, lack of knowledge and improper education leads to dangerous cases of overdose, where number of such incidents increases year after year...

LEARN MORE ABOUT HARM REDUCTION

OUR GOALS

At the very beginnig of this project we set ourselves some goals that we want to achieve with this project. Here's three main principles that we follow:

  1. EDUCATE - By providing facts and debunking myths about certain psychoactive subtances we try to teach people about risks, dangers and long-term effects of those
  2. ENTERTAIN - With our innovative procedural, cognitive NPC (Non-Player Characters) System we are making sure that every event in game will be truly uniqe on each gameplay. 
  3. REDUCE HARM -While being a funny drug dealer's simulator we also try to inform people about high risk associated with use of psychoactive substances.

OUR APPROACH

We want to hit the edutainment approach of harm reduction by entertaining people while providing true, fact-checked information about certain psychoactive substances.

This isn't an easy task, but we believe in our goal.
